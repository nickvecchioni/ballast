InfraCost GPU metrics collector installed.

The collector DaemonSet is running on GPU nodes and exposing metrics on port {{ .Values.collector.metricsPort }}.

To view raw Prometheus metrics:

  kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} \
    svc/{{ include "infracost.fullname" . }}-collector {{ .Values.collector.service.port }}:{{ .Values.collector.service.port }}

  curl http://localhost:{{ .Values.collector.service.port }}/metrics

To view per-pod GPU cost (requires kubectl-cost plugin):

  kubectl cost top pods --all-namespaces \
    --metrics-url http://localhost:{{ .Values.collector.service.port }}/metrics
